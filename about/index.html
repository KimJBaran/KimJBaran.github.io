Who is Kim Baran?

I am a Senior Software Engineer working for Meta in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. I joined them in April 2022.

My professional journey has been colorful. I’ve written C++ for embedded systems, C to create high-performance command line tools, Python/Ruby for life science data pipelines, Java/Spring for web-applications used in cancer research, and JavaScript/PHP for business applications.

Just for fun, I’m currently exploring more C++ again and I’ve taken up an interest in game development.

I’ve been late diagnosed with level 1 Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD-1, formerly “Asperger’s”). I’m fairly vocal about that to drive better accommodation for neurodivergent folks at work.
